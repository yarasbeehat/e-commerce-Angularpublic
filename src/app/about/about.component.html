About Us

ElectroStore was established in Jordan in 2007 to provide customers with comprehensive shopping experience for consumer electronics and home appliances.
ElectroStore offers all the leading international brands with the widest range of products including TV, audio systems, computers, mobile phones and IT communications, cameras, gaming, home appliances and personal care products. The product range is supported by complete after sales services such as, free home delivery and installation. ElectroStore also offers household machines and electronics repairs.

• Our Vision :
To be the leading retailer of consumer electronics, appliances and communication solutions in Jordan
• Our Mission :
To ease our clients life by providing the widest range of products under one roof, and offer after sale services anytime anywhere.
